Archival Sources

This website draws upon a multitude of sources. Thanks to the National Archives at Atlanta, drawings, plans and elevations were made available. In addition, most of the TVA generated material comes from the National Archives at Atlanta. The Cornell University Rare and Manuscript Collections digitized a few papers from the Earle Draper papers including press releases. Newspaper sources were accessed through ProQuest newspapers. The photographs come from the FSA-OWI colelction at the Library of Congress as well as other sources online.
